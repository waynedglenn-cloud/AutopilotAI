# AutoPilot AI

## Your AI Mechanic in Your Pocket

Diagnose vehicle problems, understand check engine codes, get maintenance information, and receive AI-powered repair guidance—all from your phone.

Whether you're a DIY enthusiast or simply trying to understand what's wrong with your vehicle, AutoPilot AI provides fast, easy-to-understand automotive assistance.

### Features

* 🤖 AI-powered mechanic assistant
* 🚗 Vehicle-specific maintenance information
* 🔧 OBD-II trouble code explanations
* 📸 AI photo analysis of vehicle components
* 🛠️ Tune-up specifications and maintenance schedules
* ⚡ Personalized recommendations based on your vehicle
* 💡 Beginner-friendly repair guidance

Download AutoPilot AI on Google Play and let AI help keep your vehicle running at its best.
